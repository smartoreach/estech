Established in 1997, the company has a rich experience in manufacturing OEM standard Work Rest Blades are tailor made as per centreless grinding process needs . For more than 24 years, our mastery lies in the precision of the blades that we manufacture or refurbish to customised requirements. We at ES Tech have a team of highly skilled technicians who make sure that each blade is manufactured to the best of the quality. We also advise clients about the best practises in accordance with their processes.

We also provide the best possible solutions to our clients' problem, based on deep research and process analysis. We use a specified grade of Tungsten Carbide Tips to manufacture all the work rest blades. This results in excellent wear resistance and longer life. To assure uniform strength across the blade, we get our blades silver blazed with modern technique. For our refurbishment work, we follow the principles to achieve precision and complete wear resistance to extend the life of the blade. We provide the right solution for the right problem.
