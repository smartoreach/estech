Work Rest Blades Service & Repairs

Are your work rest blades dull and worn out due to constant use or accidental damage? if the answer to the above question is ‘Yes’, then you are in the right place. You can send your blades for refurbishing services as per your requirements. We make sure that the blades have mirror finishing to them so that they are as good as new to enhance process efficiency and product quality. The refurbishing of work rest blades is done using cutting edge technologies to ensure optimum performance and extension of the blade’s life too.

We also undertake orders for Customized Work Rest Blades for centerless grinding machines, which get worn out after regular use and can be sent to us for refurbishment and made good as new.

The other types of work rest blades we repair are Carbide Blades, PCD Blades, Thread Rolling blades. We make sure the blades are corrosion-resistant, sturdy and with optimum sharpness.
