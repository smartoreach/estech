Customized Work Rest Blades

We manufacture customized work rest blades with any thickness including super thin with precision upto 30 microns. The length of the blades can be customized as per the clients' requirements. We have the capability to manufacture blades with a mirror finished contact surface and extremely close tolerance. Our blades can be used for feed grinding and through-feed too. The client can fill the requirement form and we assure to deliver as per your requirements.

We have a variety of Work Rest Blades based on applications that can be used for various purposes. Some of them are:

  • Blades for Imported Centreless Grinding Machines are designed and manufactured as per the product and process requirements of the machines.
  • Work rest blades customised to suit Bhagwan Sons and other branded centerless grinding machines.
  • Carbide Tipped Saw Blades are specifically made for cutting metals like steel, cast iron, copper, bronze, non-ferrous metals, aluminum alloys and many others.
  • PCD Blades can be used for cutting boards made of chipboard, fibreboard, composite materials, plastics, cement reinforced with fibres, etc. suitable for rough cut or finished cut. We also make PCD saw blades for panel sizing machines and vertical panel sizing machines.
  • Thread Rolling Blades are also made by us for all makes of thread rolling machines for rolling stainless steel workpieces and large profiles.
